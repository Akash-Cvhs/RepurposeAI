### Clinical Evidence
The clinical evidence section summarizes the results from internal clinical experiment reports:
* **Galantamine**: A 6-month extension study (n=189) showed that galantamine 16mg BID maintained cognitive benefits with a 1.9-point ADAS-Cog advantage over baseline (p=0.023) [1].
* **Rivastigmine**: A Phase III trial (n=312) demonstrated that rivastigmine transdermal patch 13.3mg/24h achieved a 2.4-point ADAS-Cog improvement vs placebo at 26 weeks (p=0.008) [2].
* **Donepezil**: A Phase IIb trial (n=247) showed that donepezil 10mg QD significantly improved ADAS-Cog scores by 2.8 points vs placebo at 24 weeks (p=0.012) [3].
